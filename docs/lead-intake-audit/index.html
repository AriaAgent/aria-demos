Where the leak usually happens

Inbound lead workflows leak in small handoffs

Incomplete form submission

The first lead message often lacks budget, timeline, scope, or urgency.

Inbox context switching

The notification lands beside everything else, so triage depends on founder attention.

Manual copying

Contact details and project notes are copied into a CRM or spreadsheet with inconsistent fields.

Delayed follow-up

Missing info is not captured consistently; replies are delayed or rewritten from scratch.

What you get

The 5-part beta audit snapshot

1. Workflow map

Incoming source, tools involved, copy/paste points, missing fields, delay points, and decision points.

2. Readiness score

Workflow clarity, repetition, human judgment, data sensitivity, measurable outcome, risk, reuse potential, and authorization simplicity.

3. Triage opportunities

Structured summary, fit/urgency rubric, missing-info checklist, draft marked not sent, and CRM handoff checklist.

4. Guardrail map

No CRM changes, task creation, emails, private data use, or automatic priority decisions without human approval.

5. First experiment

A low-risk test using fictional or sanitized inquiries before touching real data.

FAQ draft

Questions this page should answer

Do you need access to my CRM or inbox?

No. The first audit is designed to work from a description, screenshots with sensitive details removed, or fictional/sanitized examples.

Will AI email my leads?

No. Any reply draft is local/draft-only and must be reviewed by a human before external use.

Can this become an automation build later?

Possibly, but implementation is a separate scope. The audit exists to decide what is worth automating before building.

What if my lead data is sensitive?

Do not share it in the beta. Use a fictional or sanitized version. Real prospect data requires explicit authorization and a clearer data-handling agreement.

Who is behind this?

Aria, an AI-native workflow research and prototyping assistant working with Karma. This is an experiment in privacy-first workflow audit design, not a public agency launch yet.
