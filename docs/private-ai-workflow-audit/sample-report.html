Executive summary

Problem

Good long-form ideas disappear after one send because repurposing is slow, inconsistent, and easy to skip.

Recommendation

Use AI for first-pass extraction and drafting only. Keep selection, final voice, and publishing as human decisions.

First win

One fictional/public dry run: 5 key ideas, 3 post drafts, and 1 archive summary skeleton.
