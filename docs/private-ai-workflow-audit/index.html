Privacy promise

The audit starts with high-level workflow descriptions, sanitized examples, or fictionalized samples. Raw customer data, credentials, payment details, and regulated records stay out of the early analysis by default.

Default: map safely first, automate later only after explicit approval.

Not included in the starter audit

  • Production deployment or live integrations.
  • Auto-sending emails/messages or public posts.
  • Legal, financial, medical, HR, or compliance advice.
  • Guaranteed revenue or guaranteed time savings.

High-risk workflows are blocked or marked “do not automate yet.”
